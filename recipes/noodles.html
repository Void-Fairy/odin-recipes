Noodles

Description

Literally Noodles

Ingredients

  • 500 g Noodles
  • 1 liter Water
  • A Sprinkle of Salt

Steps

  1. Put the water on the stove and heat till it boils
  2. Add the sprinkle of water
  3. Add the noodles into the water
  4. Cook for 8-10 minutes,then try one with a fork and decide if its to your liking or you want to try it longer
  5. Add the toping of your linking
  6. Enjoy!
