Fried Ramen With Mushrooms, Chicken And Egg

Description

A noodle dish based on Ramen, with a strong, sweet and fragrant taste.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Green Onions
  • 500 g Ramen Noodles
  • 4 Eggs
  • 250 g Chicken Breast
  • 500 g Mushrooms of your choice
  • Soya
  • Hoisin sauce (e.g. Flying Goose Hoisin Sauce
  • Pepper
  • Pomegranate Sirup

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and green onions and fry them in olive oil on a mid to hight temperature
  2. Once the onions are gold-brown add the mushrooms
  3. Season them with pepper, Soya Sauce, Hoisin Sauce and pomegranate sirup
  4. Once completely fried, put them to the side
  5. Put the chicken breast into the pan
  6. Season it with pepper, Soya Sauce, Hoisin Sauce and pomegranate sirup
  7. Fry it till well done
  8. Put the chicken breast to the side
  9. Cook the noodles as written on the back side of the package
  10. Put the noodles into a pan with olive oil, then add mushrooms and chicken breast
  11. Fry it on mid temperature
  12. Cook the eggs 4.5 minutes (cook it soft on the inside)
  13. Put the eggs on the dish
  14. Enjoy!
