Mac'n Cheese

Description

A noodle dish, suitable for those who truly enjoy their cheese!

It is made of noodles and a lot of cheese. Variation can even be created by switching out the types of noodles and trying out different cheese.

Ingredients

  • 300 g noodles of your liking
  • 600 ml milk
  • 3 tbsp flour
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 500 g cheese of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles as written on the back
  2. Slowly melt the butter in the pot
  3. Add the flour while stirring it
  4. Let the mix become slightly golden
  5. Add milk slowly under active stirring
  6. Add the grated cheese and let it melt
  7. Once creamy, add the noodles into the sauce and mix
  8. Put the mixture into a backing mold of suitable size
  9. Bake for around 20 minutes on 180Â°C until the cheese is golden brown
